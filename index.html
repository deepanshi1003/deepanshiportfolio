Case Study
Growing an agri-investment platform's organic search from zero

An agri-investment platform targeting land buyers and NRIs across India had no organic search presence when I joined. I built their SEO strategy from scratch — keyword research, content production, on-page optimisation, and off-page outreach.

The Challenge

The agri-investment space is niche but competitive. The site needed to rank for both informational queries (land laws, farming types, investment benefits) and transactional ones (buying land in specific regions) — with no existing content, no backlinks, and no domain authority.

What I Did
  • Content strategy Mapped keywords across agriculture, real estate, and investment verticals. Produced 44+ SEO-optimised blogs and landing pages targeting high-intent queries.
  • On-page SEO Optimised meta titles, descriptions, internal linking, and heading structure using RankMath. Each page targeted a distinct primary keyword.
  • Keyword wins Ranked a competitive transactional keyword on Page 1 of Google, alongside multiple long-tail informational queries driving steady impressions.
  • Off-page SEO Built backlinks through outreach, directory submissions, and guest posting to grow domain authority.
  • WordPress management Updated page layouts via Elementor, improved UX and mobile responsiveness, and published all content directly in the CMS.
  • SEMrush audits Ran regular technical audits, tracked keyword rankings, and used competitor gap analysis to find new content opportunities.
